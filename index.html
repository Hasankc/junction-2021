Intelligent Energy Saver End-users Engagement Feature

The end-users are engaged in the energy-saving process by a new feature that shows the amount of energy saved and CO2 emissions reduced by using the Intelligent Energy Saver in the Radio Access Network (RAN). The feature is added to the operators' platforms (e.g. OmaElisa)

Vendor’s static energy-saving settings are not sufficiently turning network elements ‘off’ when not in use. Elisa Polystar’s Intelligent Energy Saver takes the customers into account when turning off the base station and saves up to 14% of the energy consumption.
